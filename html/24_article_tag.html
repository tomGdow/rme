article
The HTML article element represents a self-contained composition in a document, page, application, or site, which is intended to be independently distributable or reusable (e.g., in syndication ).
Examples include:
  1. a forum post
  2. a magazine or newspaper article
  3. a blog entry
  4. a user-submitted comment
  5. an interactive widget or gadget
  6. any other independent item of content
    7. .
A given document can have multiple articles in it
