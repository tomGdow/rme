Notes
rowspan
colspan
Make it possible for table data elements to span across columns and rows
vertical-align
May take values of top, middle and bottom
text-align
May take values of left center, and right
table-layout
Use table-layout:fixed for faster rendering of tables
tfoot
In HTML-5, must go after tbody (Otherwise, will not validate)
colgroup
Group together columns of data, typically for formatting purposes.
May use span attribute or <col>
The <col> element is void element and enclosing colgroup must not have a span attribute (but <col> may take span attribute. )
col has no end tag and must be a child of colgroup which has no span attribute
table border
Both border:none and border:0 are acceptable ways of not showing a border (but they do subtly different things: border:0 sets the value of border-width to zero.
border-collapse
Possible values are collapse and separate
empty-cells
Possible values are hide and show
border-spacing
May take two values (space-separated) representing horizontal and vertical spacing.
caption
Specifies table title.
Note the css rule caption-side which may take values of top and bottom
Mnemonic
