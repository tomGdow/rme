Entity
A HTML entity is a piece of text ("String") that begins with an ampersand (&) and ends with a semicolon ( ; )
Frequently used to display reserved characters (which are parsed as HTML) and invisible characters such as non-breaking space;
Syntax
  1. &entity_name;
  2. &#entity_number;
Examples
  1. &amp; or &#38; (Ampersand, &)
  2. &lt; or &#60; (less-than, < )
  3. &gt; or &#62; (greater-than, > )
  4. &quot; or &#34; (quotation, " )
